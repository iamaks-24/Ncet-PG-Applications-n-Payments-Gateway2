Application Form Fee (Non-Refundable)

  • Indian: 500/-
  • NRI: 2000/-
  • Foreign National: USD 50
Fee Structure:
Year-Wise Payment Amount (In Rs)
1st Year Booking Amount at the time of joining 25,000
Balance Semester Amount to be paid within 2 weeks of seat booking As per fee structure
Second-semester fees to be paid before the beginning of the second semester As per fee structure
Second year onwards, the full semester fee is to be paid before the commencement of every semester As per fee structure

Terms & Conditions

Reporting, Refund and Cancellation Policy:
  • Reporting must be done within 2 weeks of seat booking or else the seat automatically becomes eligible for direct cancellation.
  • Any amount paid below Rs. 15,000 is non-refundable.
  • Any amount paid above Rs. 15,000 will be refunded after deducting a cancellation charge of Rs. 15,000.
  • Any Candidate who does not complete the reporting procedure within two weeks of seat booking, the seat automatically becomes eligible for direct cancellation.
  • No refund will be provided if a candidate cancels the seat after reporting. Only documents will be handed over in such cases.
  • Note: Admission is provisional and subject to approval of Bengaluru City University.
  • Incomplete applications are liable to be rejected.
